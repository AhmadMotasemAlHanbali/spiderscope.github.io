About SpiderScope

From PCB to Product - All under one roof

Who We Are

SpiderScope is a hardware-focused IoT startup. We design and manufacture custom IoT devices for factories, farms, and vehicles. We own the entire pipeline: PCB design with KiCad, 3D printing, firmware development, and cloud integration.

We are not a PowerPoint company. We have an office, a car for logistics, 3D printers (FDM & Resin), and in-house hardware engineers. Our MVP is ready: an ESP32-S3 camera module with custom PCB and 3D printed enclosure.

Our mission: Make custom IoT hardware accessible, branded, and deployable within 48 hours.
