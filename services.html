Our Services

End-to-end IoT hardware solutions from PCB to product

Custom PCB Design with KiCad

Professional PCB design using KiCad (open-source, 3D enabled). We create branded boards with your logo on the silkscreen layer. Compatible with ESP32, STM32, and custom MCUs. We export STEP files for perfect 3D printed enclosure fit.

  • KiCad 3D visualization
  • Silkscreen branding with logo
  • STEP file export for enclosures
  • NRE: $500 - $2000

ESP32 Camera Solutions

ESP32-S3 and ESP32-CAM from Espressif. OV2640 and OV5640 cameras. Perfect for industrial vision, farm monitoring, and vehicle cameras. Video streaming, motion detection, and AI on the edge.

  • ESP32-S3 (WiFi, BLE, AI)
  • OV2640 / OV5640 cameras
  • Video streaming to cloud
  • Custom firmware development

3D Printed Enclosures

In-house 3D printing with FDM and Resin printers. We design custom enclosures that perfectly fit your PCB and camera modules. Your logo embossed or printed on the case. Rapid prototyping within 24 hours.

  • FDM for prototypes
  • Resin for final products
  • Logo embossing on case
  • 24-hour turnaround

Firmware & Cloud Integration

We write custom firmware for ESP32 using ESP-IDF, Arduino, or MicroPython. Connect your devices to any cloud platform via MQTT, HTTP, or LoRa. Custom dashboards for remote monitoring and control.

  • ESP-IDF / Arduino / MicroPython
  • MQTT, HTTP, LoRa, BLE
  • Custom cloud dashboards
  • Over-the-air (OTA) updates

Full Branding (PCB + Case)

We don't just assemble. We brand every device with your company logo on both the PCB silkscreen and the 3D printed enclosure. Your customers receive a professional, branded product ready for market.

  • Logo on PCB silkscreen layer
  • Logo embossed on 3D case
  • Custom packaging available
  • White-label ready
